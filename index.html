Short Bio

Baoyu Zhou is a Ph.D. candidate at Lehigh University, expected to graduate in 2022. He received his Bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2016 and his Master degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISE) from Lehigh University in 2018. He was a Givens Associate in the Mathematics and Computer Science Division at Argonne National Laboratory and a Research Intern at Facebook AI Research. He won the Van Hoesen Family Best Publication Award at Lehigh ISE Department in 2021. He was a Program Committee member of NeurIPS Workshops NFFL-2021, OPT-2021, and OPT-2020. His research focuses on developing, analyzing, and implementing practical algorithms for solving large-scale nonlinear optimization problems.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Large-scale Nonlinear Optimisation

  • Constrained Stochastic Optimization

  • Nonsmooth Optimization

  • Optimization Methods for Machine Learning and Other Applications
